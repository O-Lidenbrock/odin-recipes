Omelette Recipe

Description

Perfect for a late Saturday morning, with the mixings from last night's salad, this omelette recipe is quick enough to be made before a busy workday. In a cast iron skillet this recipe browns beautifully, and the cirular pan helps fold it perfectly

Prep time is about 10 minutes, depending what you cut up. I like to add some leeks and cubed mozzarella which takes more time. Cook time is about 5 min

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • bacon bits
  • cheddar cheese (substitute with mozzarella or colby to taste)
  • peppers
  • green onions
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. crack eggs into a bowl and stir vigorously till yolk and white mix
  2. stir in add-ins
  3. cook in a skillet about 2.5 minutes, then fold in half and flip
  4. add salt and pepper to taste
